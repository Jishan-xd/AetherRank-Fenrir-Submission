SCOPE OF WORK

AetherRank is an AI-powered esports platform that helps players improve their skills, participate in tournaments, build teams, and track their gaming performance.

Key Features

* User Registration and Login
* Player Profile Management
* Tournament Creation and Participation
* Team Creation and Recruitment
* Global and Regional Leaderboards
* Match History and Performance Analytics
* Achievement and Reward System
* AetherMind™ AI Gaming Companion

AetherMind Feature

AetherMind™ learns from a player's previous matches and identifies recurring mistakes. During live gameplay, it provides personalized suggestions and strategic guidance to help players avoid repeating errors. The AI continuously improves its recommendations based on player performance and match history.

User Roles

* Player
* Team Manager
* Administrator

Requirements

* Secure user authentication
* Responsive web interface
* Real-time leaderboard updates
* Tournament management functionality
* AI-powered performance insights
* Match analytics and history tracking

Deliverable

A responsive esports platform with tournament management, team recruitment, player analytics, leaderboards, and an adaptive AI coaching system.
